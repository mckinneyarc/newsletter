üåç Join Us!

The McKinney Amateur Radio Club (MARC) welcomes everyone, whether you're just discovering the world of Amateur Radio or you're an experienced operator looking to connect with fellow enthusiasts. Our club is open to anyone with a passion for radio communication, a curiosity for learning, or a desire to expand their skills. Amateur Radio is a fascinating, educational, and rewarding hobby, and we invite you to explore everything MARC has to offer.

As a MARC member, you‚Äôll have access to a variety of engaging activities and resources, including regular meetings, special events, and hands-on community service projects. MARC E-MAIL REFLECTOR ‚Äî GROUPS.IO

MARC maintains a chat group on groups.io to facilitate club communication between meetings and newsletters. Sign up for this service by visiting https://groups.io/g/McKinneyARC. Your application will be approved quickly.

The purposes of the MARC email reflector are: 1) Facilitate the timely distribution of MARC information and news to club members. 2) Allow for the sharing of amateur radio related topics among club members. 3) Provide a forum for the discussion of amateur related information among club members. 4) Act as a marketplace for the buying and selling of amateur radio and computer equipment and services. Club members should utilize good judgment with regard to topics or content that are posted on the MARC reflector.
