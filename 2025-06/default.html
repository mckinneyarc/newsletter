üåç Join Us!

The McKinney Amateur Radio Club (MARC) welcomes everyone, whether you're just discovering the world of Amateur Radio or you're an experienced operator looking to connect with fellow enthusiasts. Our club is open to anyone with a passion for radio communication, a curiosity for learning, or a desire to expand their skills. üîß Electronics 101 ‚Äì Hands-On DC Circuits

The first class in the Play 'n Learn series ‚ÄúElectronics 101 ‚Äì Hands On‚Äù will meet on Saturday, June 14, 2025, from 9AM to 12PM at McKinney Fire Station 10, 1150 Olympic Crossing, McKinney, TX 75071.

All club members and anyone interested in electronics is invited. There is no charge for the class, and all materials, equipment, and handouts are provided.

This session, DC Circuits, led by Bob DeVance, K5CRX, will cover basic components and their use in direct current (DC) circuits. Components, meters, wiring, and breadboards will be provided to support the hands-on format. As each topic is discussed, participants will actively work with the concepts and components.

Topics Covered

  • Current, voltage, and resistance
  • Ohm‚Äôs Law and its application
  • Resistors, lamps, switches, and batteries
  • Reading and understanding color codes

If you have a multimeter or component tester, please bring it. If not, class-provided meters will be available.

The class ends at noon, and participants are welcome to join for lunch at a nearby restaurant.

Follow-on sessions covering AC Circuits, Solid State Devices, and Digital Electronics are planned for the coming months.
