📢 President's Message

Hello MARC Family,

May is off to a fantastic start, and the weather has certainly delivered! We kicked things off with our Potluck POTA BBQ at Bonham State Park, enjoying perfect conditions, incredible food, great fellowship, and plenty of fun. A big thank-you to everyone who joined us and brought a dish to share, it truly made the event special.

We were also excited to have Frank (KG5AHJ) from YouTube channel Tank Radio stop by and spend time with us. It was especially meaningful to see members travel from long distances to attend, something that speaks volumes about the passion and camaraderie within our hobby. And of course, several attendees took on the challenge of tracking down three radio foxes throughout the park!

Our spring POTA event always serves as a great warm-up for ARRL Field Day, and preparations are already well underway. Our dedicated Field Day Director, Michael (W5MEF), has been hard at work organizing everything for next month's event. That includes coordinating this year's custom MARC Field Day t-shirts, and we've already surpassed previous order records! If you'd like one, be sure to reach out to Michael soon. And yes, you read that right, this year's Field Day will include air conditioning! We're all looking forward to operating in comfort while still enjoying the full experience.

Meanwhile, the Fairview Repeater Project continues to gain strong momentum. The generosity of our members has been outstanding, with many stepping up through both financial contributions and hands-on support. There's still work to be done, and additional help is needed. If you'd like to get involved, please reach out to Dick (N5RKP). You'll also find more details in this newsletter and at our upcoming general meeting.

As we move through May, we also take time to reflect on the meaning of Memorial Day by honoring the brave men and women who gave their lives in service to our country. MARC is proud to continue our partnership with Smiles Charity, which provides mortgage-free homes for wounded veterans. We'll be assisting with their Boots & Bling event on Sunday, May 24th, featuring Grammy-nominated artist Pat Green. If you're interested in volunteering, please reach out to Sid (K5DKW), and stay tuned for more details at our May general meeting.

Finally, don't forget about our Saturday morning breakfast at IHOP McKinney. Come out around 7:00 AM (or earlier) to grab a seat, enjoy a great meal, and connect with fellow operators. It's a perfect way to start your weekend.

— Robert Keister, WZ5V
2026 MARC President

🌍 Join Us!

The McKinney Amateur Radio Club (MARC) welcomes everyone, whether you're just discovering the world of Amateur Radio or you're an experienced operator looking to connect with fellow enthusiasts. Our club is open to anyone with a passion for radio communication, a curiosity for learning, or a desire to expand their skills. Amateur Radio is a fascinating, educational, and rewarding hobby, and we invite you to explore everything MARC has to offer.

As a MARC member, you’ll have access to a variety of engaging activities and resources, including regular meetings, special events, and hands-on community service projects. These events allow members to enhance their radio skills, contribute to emergency preparedness initiatives, and build meaningful connections within the amateur radio community. Joining MARC is a great way to grow personally while making a positive impact.

Interested in becoming a member? We'd love to have you! Feel free to reach out to our club secretary at secretary@mckinneyarc.org for more details. You can also visit our website at mckinneyarc.org to access a membership application and learn more about the benefits of joining, such as access to club resources, participation in exclusive events, and opportunities to collaborate with fellow operators.

If you're not ready to join just yet, why not attend one of our monthly meetings or public events? These gatherings provide a fantastic way to meet club members, ask questions, and experience the excitement of amateur radio firsthand.

Prefer to reach us by mail? Feel free to contact us at:

Secretary, McKinney Amateur Radio Club
P.O. Box 267
McKinney, TX 75070

We look forward to hearing from you and hope to see you at an upcoming meeting or event. Join us and become part of a passionate and welcoming community dedicated to the world of Amateur Radio!

💰 April 2026 Financial Report

The club treasury remains in solid shape.

We've taken a proactive approach to fundraising by enrolling in several ongoing community programs. Walmart's Round Up program allows shoppers to round up their purchases to the nearest dollar, donating the spare change to the club. Kroger Community Rewards provides donations based on members' everyday grocery purchases when linked to our organization. Through Walmart's Spark Good program, we're able to receive grants, donations, and visibility within their charitable platform. These programs generate small, but steady support with minimal effort from members.

Several fundraisers have been organized with local restaurants where they return a portion of sales to the club just from patrons identifying they are supporting the club prior to ordering. Stay informed with club emails, our website calendar and our Discord events channel for upcoming events and fundraisers. Your participation is appreciated.

Many of our members stepped up by donating needed items for the UHF/VHF kits, which significantly reduced out-of-pocket costs for the club. The kits look fantastic and will serve the club well at events for years to come.

In addition, we are making full use of our tax-exempt status to reduce costs on purchases and we've been working with local vendors to secure in-kind donations of equipment and materials—helping lower overall project expenses.

With member approval of the Fairview Tower Repeater project you are seeing requests for donations of labor, materials and financial support. Any assistance is appreciated and projects go faster with help. Dick Price (N5RKP) is managing the project and a list of needs was sent out.

If you need assistance signing up for Kroger or Walmart email treasurer@mckinneyarc.org.

MARC E-MAIL REFLECTOR — GROUPS.IO

MARC maintains a chat group on groups.io to facilitate club communication between meetings and newsletters. Sign up for this service by visiting https://groups.io/g/McKinneyARC. Your application will be approved quickly.

The purposes of the MARC email reflector are: 1) Facilitate the timely distribution of MARC information and news to club members. 2) Allow for the sharing of amateur radio related topics among club members. 3) Provide a forum for the discussion of amateur related information among club members. 4) Act as a marketplace for the buying and selling of amateur radio and computer equipment and services. Club members should utilize good judgment with regard to topics or content that are posted on the MARC reflector. To that end it is recommended that political and religious topics or content be avoided unless directly related to amateur radio. In addition, personal attacks in any form will not be tolerated.
