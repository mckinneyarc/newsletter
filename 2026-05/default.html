📢 President's Message

Hello MARC Members,

Spring has arrived, and April is already upon us! With the warmer weather and longer days, it's the perfect time to get outside, enjoy time with family, and get on the air. Whether you're heading out for a POTA activation or relaxing on the back patio calling CQ and making new contacts, there are plenty of opportunities to enjoy the hobby. Hopefully, a few April showers will help wash away that stubborn, sticky yellow pollen, especially the layer covering our antennas!

A big thank you to everyone who attended the VHF/UHF Go-Kit build in Anna on Saturday, March 28th. It was a great event filled with learning, teamwork, and fun. Special appreciation goes to Lance – KM6LMP for hosting and leading such a successful project.

As always, MARC has an active schedule ahead. Here are a couple of upcoming events to mark on your calendar:

Heart of Melissa Spring Fest

Join us at the MARC booth on Saturday, April 11th, at the Heart of Melissa Spring Fest, hosted by the Melissa Chamber of Commerce. This is a great opportunity to connect with the community and share the excitement of amateur radio. If you're interested in volunteering, please reach out to Lance – KM6LMP.

Potluck POTA BBQ at Bonham State Park

Mark your calendars for Saturday, May 2, starting at 10:00 AM, and join us for a Potluck POTA BBQ at the group pavilion. We'll have club go-kits available, so feel free to operate without bringing your own equipment, or bring your gear if you prefer. The club call sign will be available for use, giving technicians a chance to get on HF, or you're welcome to operate under your own call sign within your privileges.

Melinda and I will be bringing slow-smoked pork loins and BBQ favorites. Please bring a dish to share and come ready for great food, fellowship, and radio fun. Family members and guests are always welcome! And don't forget your handheld—Felicia the Fox is expected to make another appearance somewhere in the park.

As spring weather can change quickly, this is also a great time to review your emergency preparedness plans. Make sure your equipment is ready to monitor repeaters when Skywarn and Collin ARES are activated. Staying prepared helps ensure we can support our community when it matters most.

We're looking forward to a fantastic season ahead. Thank you for being part of the MARC community and we sincerely appreciate each and every one of you.

— Robert Keister, WZ5V
2026 MARC President

🌍 Join Us!

The McKinney Amateur Radio Club (MARC) welcomes everyone, whether you're just discovering the world of Amateur Radio or you're an experienced operator looking to connect with fellow enthusiasts. Our club is open to anyone with a passion for radio communication, a curiosity for learning, or a desire to expand their skills. Amateur Radio is a fascinating, educational, and rewarding hobby, and we invite you to explore everything MARC has to offer.

As a MARC member, you’ll have access to a variety of engaging activities and resources, including regular meetings, special events, and hands-on community service projects. These events allow members to enhance their radio skills, contribute to emergency preparedness initiatives, and build meaningful connections within the amateur radio community. Joining MARC is a great way to grow personally while making a positive impact.

Interested in becoming a member? We'd love to have you! Feel free to reach out to our club secretary at secretary@mckinneyarc.org for more details. You can also visit our website at mckinneyarc.org to access a membership application and learn more about the benefits of joining, such as access to club resources, participation in exclusive events, and opportunities to collaborate with fellow operators.

If you're not ready to join just yet, why not attend one of our monthly meetings or public events? These gatherings provide a fantastic way to meet club members, ask questions, and experience the excitement of amateur radio firsthand.

Prefer to reach us by mail? Feel free to contact us at:

Secretary, McKinney Amateur Radio Club
P.O. Box 267
McKinney, TX 75070

We look forward to hearing from you and hope to see you at an upcoming meeting or event. Join us and become part of a passionate and welcoming community dedicated to the world of Amateur Radio!

📞 Update Your Emergency Contact Information

At the December board meeting, Lance proposed maintaining emergency contact information for members in case of incidents during field events or club activities. Good news - this feature already exists in our Ham Club Online system!

To ensure your emergency contact information is current, please sign into your profile at HamClubOnline.com and follow the steps below.

Here's how to add your emergency contact:

1. Select Manage Your Club Profile off the main menu.

2. Scroll down to the section titled Roster Information and enter your desired contact's name and phone number in the Emergency Contact field.

This information will help ensure member safety during field events and club activities. We encourage all members to update their profiles with this important information.

MARC E-MAIL REFLECTOR — GROUPS.IO

MARC maintains a chat group on groups.io to facilitate club communication between meetings and newsletters. Sign up for this service by visiting https://groups.io/g/McKinneyARC. Your application will be approved quickly.

The purposes of the MARC email reflector are: 1) Facilitate the timely distribution of MARC information and news to club members. 2) Allow for the sharing of amateur radio related topics among club members. 3) Provide a forum for the discussion of amateur related information among club members. 4) Act as a marketplace for the buying and selling of amateur radio and computer equipment and services. Club members should utilize good judgment with regard to topics or content that are posted on the MARC reflector. To that end it is recommended that political and religious topics or content be avoided unless directly related to amateur radio. In addition, personal attacks in any form will not be tolerated.
